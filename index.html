Rules and Regulations


  • Two events - DevJam and CodeStart - will be conducted under Weekend of Code 2k24.
  • It is upto you whether you want to register in both the events or any single event.
  • No two teams should have same members.
  • Inter-branch (B.Tech) teams are allowed.
  • All the members of team must be from same year.
  • All the members of team must be from same course (either B.Tech or MCA).
  • You can create different teams for both the events.
  • Deadline for registration is - 4th April 2024 11:59PM.
  • Weekend of Code 2k24 is scheduled from 6th April - 12th April 2024.

About the Event

Weekend of Code, the annual technical event of MNNIT, Allahabad is up with its 2k24 edition! The enthralling week long event consists of DevJam and CodeStart. DevJam aims at providing a glimpse of the world of development to our freshmen while CodeStart is a coding event for our freshmen and sophomores. The sole purpose of this event is to provide participants with hands-on experience and exposure along with proper guidance. Get ready for an adventurous journey of learning, innovating and exploring new technologies.

  • Week dedicated to coding and development
  • Meet Developers and Coders from your batch
  • Get Inspired by Amazing Mentors
  • Make project in any language you want
  • Test your coding skills with 5 hour insomaniac event CodeStart

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN

Participating in WoC will go a long way towards developing your technical skills. This event will be a great opportunity for freshmen to get started with developing and coding. You don't need any prior knowledge to participate in this event (except zeal for learning!!) because your mentor will guide you through every step. At the end of this event you will have a great project. Coding and developing skills will also help you in getting an internship and placement. Don't miss out this opportunity and discover the fun in coding and developing.

Introduction to Open Source

Get yourself introduced to Open Source Communities and be a part of them. Feel proud to be a member of communities like GitHub, Python Software Foundation, etc.

Intro to Competitive Programming

Get yourself introduced to Competitive Programming. Sharpen your competitive programming skills by competing against brightest minds of college.

Projects for your Resume

Enhance your Resume with exciting projects developed under the guidance of the Mentors, having expertise in their respective fields of development.

    Judges will start evaluating the projects.
